Professor

Eunho Yang

eunhoy@kaist.ac.kr

Dr. Eunho Yang is a tenured associate professor in the Graduate School of AI and School of Computing at KAIST. Before joining KAIST in the summer of 2016, he spent two years as a research staff member at IBM T.J. Watson Research Center. He obtained his Ph.D. in 2014 from the University of Texas at Austin and did M.S. and B.S. from the Seoul National University in 2006 and 2004, respectively. From 2006 to 2009, he was a researcher at Korea Telecom as an alternative military service in Korea. His research interest includes general statistical machine learning with a special focus on high-dimensional statistics and diverse machine learning algorithms with the recent focus on the generative models.
