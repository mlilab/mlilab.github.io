For Prospective Students

We are looking for highly self-motivated students for all levels including M.S. students, Ph.D. students, and post doctors. We recommend prospective M.S. students to start as undergraduate interns first for at least one semester. If you are interested in MLILAB, please get in touch with the professor via email.

Notice for Korean military-sponsored scholarship students
우리 연구실은 현재 군 위탁 장학생을 모집하고 있습니다. 우리는 군에 실질적인 도움이 될 수 있는 연구 경험을 제공합니다. 구체적으로 object detection, autonomous driving, domain adaptation, debiasing 등과 같은 군대에서 직접적으로 활용할 수 있는 연구를 진행하고 있습니다. 관심이 있으시다면 언제든지 연락해 주시면 감사하겠습니다.

Notice on the location of our lab
Our lab is currently located in both Seongnam and Daejeon. However, our lab is relocating from Daejeon to the metropolitan area including Seongnam and Seoul, and most of the students have already moved to Seongnam. Unless there are special circumstances, new members are expected to join us in the metropolitan area.
